# AI-Driven Drone Autonomy: A Comprehensive Study

Welcome to my aerospace engineering research repository. Over the next few months, I am mapping out how machine learning algorithms optimize flight paths, process sensor fusion data, and interact with fluid dynamics (CFD) in autonomous unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs).

## 🚀 Key Research Areas

### 1. Flight Path Planning & Collision Avoidance
* **The Concept:** Traditional drones rely on static GPS coordinates. Modern autonomous drones use AI algorithms like Deep Reinforcement Learning (DRL) to adapt to real-time wind gusts and dodge moving obstacles.
* **Core Algorithms:** A* Search, Dijkstra’s, and Q-Learning.

### 2. Sensor Fusion & Avionics
* Drones process thousands of data points per second from accelerometers, gyroscopes, and LiDAR.
* AI helps filter out "noise" from wind vibrations to give the flight controller a perfectly accurate picture of where the drone is in 3D space.
